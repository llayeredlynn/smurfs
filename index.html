The Smurfs

Who are they? What do they do? How do they live?

Welcome to Smurf Living!

We are giving you an inside look into the The Smurfs!
The Smurfs are a fictional colony of small blue human-like creatures who live in mushroom-shaped houses in the forest. There are 105 Smurfs in Smurf Village. Papa Smurf is the leader of the village and over 500 years old! Most Smurfs are around 100 years old and live simple lives with lots of adventure. The Smurfs love to eat Sarsaparilla leaves and berries, the secret to their long lives and strong health. Money does not exist in Smurf Village, instead each Smurf shares what they are good at with the other Smurfs and in return is given their necessities of life including housing, clothes and food.
Fun fact: The Smurfs wear Phrygian Caps sometimes called liberty caps and in artistic representations they signify freedom and the pursuit of liberty.
