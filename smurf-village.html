Smurf Village

The Smurfs live in mushroom-shaped houses in the forest, the exact location of which is unknown to all but Smurfs and Mother Nature. As noted before, there are 100+ Smurfs in Smurf Village. The most prominent Smurfs are Papa Smurf, Smurfette, Baker Smurf, Hefty Smurf, Vanity Smurf, Brainy Smurf and Painter Smurf. Most Smurfs are around 100 years old and live simple lives, but love adventure. The Smurfs favorite food is Sarsaparilla leaves and berries, the secret to their long lives and strong health. Money does not exist in Smurf Village, instead each Smurf shares what they are good at with the other Smurfs and in return is given their necessities of life including housing, clothes and food.
