Meet the Smurfs

The Smurfs are a fictional colony of small blue human-like creatures who live in mushroom-shaped houses in the forest. Most recently, movies have been created to tell the Smurfs story. Check out The Smurfs, The Smurfs 2, and Smurfs: The Lost Village to see more Smurfy goodness. Below we have listed some of our favorite Smurf characters, known and less well-known.

Papa Smurf

Papa Smurf is the leader, father, chief and king of the Smurfs, and can be easily distinguished by his red clothes and bushy white beard. Smurfs turn to Papa Smurf when things go awry. Papa Smurf is skilled in making magical spells and potions.

Brainy Smurf

Brainy Smurf considers himself the most intelligent Smurf in the village and an expert on everything, although his actual knowledge is questionable at best; often, his attempts to help in a given situation lead to more problems. He can be distinguished from the others by the large glasses he wears.

Baker Smurf

Baker Smurf is the local baker of the village. He directs a bakery shop and provides fresh bread for the Smurfs.

Smurfette

Smurfette is a female Smurf who was created by Gargamel. She originally had black hair, though this turned blonde when Papa Smurf worked all night to make her a real Smurf.

Hiker Smurf

Hiker Smurf has a flower in his hat, a green backpack on his back and a walking stick. He loves to hike the forest in search of adventure.

Vanity Smurf

Vanity Smurf is the epitome of a Narcissist. He has a flower in his hat, and he often holds a hand mirror, staring into his own reflection, which he kisses often.

Baby Smurf

Baby Smurf was brought by a stork on a night of a blue moon to the Smurf village. He has magical powers and, like Earth babies, he babbles, laughs, and cries.

Carpenter Smurf

Carpenter Smurf only appears in the comic books, however he provides wood for the construction projects within Smurf Village.

Greedy Smurf

Greedy Smurf is an exceptionally gluttonous Smurf. He has an exceptional interest in eating cakes and other pastry. Greedy usually cannot seem to help himself, and he is often chased by Chef Smurf for stealing snacks and food.

Postman Smurf

Postman Smurf only appears in the comic books, however he is the local postal carrier around Smurf Village.
